Where to find us

The museum is located at Kaigaten 8, 5016 Bergen.

Admission

Admission is free for all

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. There are also three wheelchairs on site for visitors if needed. The museum has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired. You can easily bring a service dog to the museum, as long as you have your permit card readily available.

Food & drink

There is a café attached to the museum where you can enjoy light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more. We also offer a range of gluten free alternatives. Our café follows the seasons and holidays, so look out for something seasonal and tasty all year round!

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum. We also offer gift cards which can be used on anything, anywhere on site.
